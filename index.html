About Me

Hey there! I'm Alex and I enjoy exploring and creating new things. Before college, I had always been interested in technology's automation and video games but never learned how to code or ever had a thought about web designs.

During my third year of college, I decided to join a designathon for fun and that's when I discovered my passion for web development and UX/UI design. Now, instead of looking at a website for its content, I find myself analyzing its design and functionality.

While I have a variety of non-technical skills, adaptability stands out as one of my strongest traits. Throughout my academic and personal experiences, I've always accepted change and new challenges. From learning new technologies and environments, adjusting to different team dynamics, or navigating through obstacles, I've found that adaptability allows me to remain focused, resilient, and open to growth in any situation.
